About Egypt

Located in the northeast corner of Africa, Egypt is a country steeped in history and mystery. With a population of over 100 million people, it is the most populous country in the Arab world. Egypt is famed for its ancient civilization, with a history that spans over 5,000 years. Visitors can marvel at the architectural feats of the Pharaohs, explore the treasures of the Nile River, and immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and traditions that continue to thrive today.

Delve into Ancient Wonders

Witness the Great Pyramids of Giza: Stand at the foot of these colossal structures, a testament to human ingenuity, and ponder the mysteries they hold.

Unveiling Cairo's Charm

Wander Through Khan el-Khalili Bazaar: Lose yourself in the labyrinthine alleys of this historic bazaar, a shopper's paradise offering spices, perfumes, handcrafts, and souvenirs.

Beyond the Tourist Trail

Hike to Where Faith Began: Embark on a challenging yet rewarding hike up Mount Sinai, following in the footsteps of Moses and witnessing a breathtaking sunrise from the summit.
